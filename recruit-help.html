How does the free tier work?
Free includes unlimited roles and unlimited applicants — every applicant to your role gets AI-screened and surfaced. AI talent search across our broader network is capped at 3 results, the interview plan is limited to 2 rounds, and EOR hiring is Pro-only. Upgrade to Pro ($99/mo) for unlimited AI search, deeper interview plans, white-label careers page, India EOR + e-sign offer letters.
Where do candidates come from?
Three sources: (1) applicants from your shareable career link, (2) our talent network, and (3) AI-sourced from major Indian platforms. All go through the same vetting before reaching your pipeline.
What does AI screening check?
Resume + skills match against your JD, screening Q answers, a 3-min async video for communication + thinking, English fluency, and a role-specific rubric (e.g. system design for engineering roles).
Is India EOR really included?
Yes — on the Pro plan. Once a candidate accepts, we handle payroll, PF, gratuity, ESI, income tax, contracts and Form 16. You see one consolidated monthly invoice.
Can I bring my own ATS / pipeline?
Greenhouse and Ashby integrations are on the roadmap. For now, exported candidate data is available as CSV/JSON from any role.
How do refunds work?
14-day money-back if your first hire doesn't work out. Cancel anytime — no annual lock-in unless you choose the annual plan (which is 20% off).
