Continuous learning loop

Every decision the client makes feeds back into the model. Rejected profiles down-weight similar candidates; hired profiles up-weight matching traits. Each subsequent shortlist for the same role gets sharper — without the client tuning anything.

Why this wins

  • Client touches the system 3 times: publish, approve forward, approve offer
  • AI runs the entire first round — no calendar Tetris for the client
  • Auto-stage transitions cut kanban toil to near zero
  • Same effort whether hiring 1 person or 50
